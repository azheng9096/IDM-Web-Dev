INTO TIME.COM

Artwork | Net Art | Neen

  • initially created in 2010 with Flash
  • later migrated to JavaScript
  • marked Rozendaal's transition from figurative to abstract browser-based compositions
  • explores browser's potentiality in portraying aesthetics through a simple and interactive experience
  • requires viewer's participation through clicking on the gradient grids in the piece, which splits the gradient into two
  • study of time and space with browser art - showcases its fluidity
